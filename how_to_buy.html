Foreigners
  • Those who have a health insurance premium status, please bring your health insurance card to a health insurance designated pharmacy to purchase it.
  • Those without health insurance coverage: foreigners, foreign (mainland) spouses, Lu Sheng, etc., please purchase a residence permit or entry and exit permit.

Purchasing rules are the same as those of natives.

Each person can purchase 2 masks per card (card), and they cannot buy again within 7 days.

Residence permits or entry and exit permits with double numbers at the end (0, 2, 4, 6, 8) can be purchased on Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday; single numbers (1, 3, 5, 7, 9) can be purchased at Buy Monday, Wednesday, and Friday; Sunday is open for everyone to buy.

If people have questions about masks, they can call the special line 1922, 1919, or go to the Central Health Insurance Agency's Global Information Network (www.nhi.gov.tw) to inquire about the special health insurance pharmacy. 598 or 4128-678 (without adding area code), change the phone to 02-4128-678.
